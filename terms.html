Tearms & Condition

    Payment Terms :

  • Clients are required to pay at least 50% of the total package fee at the time of booking to confirm the reservation. The remaining balance must be cleared before or on the day of the event.
    Clients may choose to pay the full package fee at the time of booking if preferred.
    Payments can be made via bank transfer, online payment, or cash.

    • Order Cancellation & Refund Policy :

  • In case of cancellation, a cancellation charge of 15% to 20% of the total package fee will be deducted from the refund.
    To avoid cancellation charges, clients must notify Studio97 at least one to 1.5 months in advance of the scheduled event.
    If a cancellation is made within a month of the event, the advance payment will not be refundable.
    Date rescheduling is subject to availability and may incur an additional fee.

    • On-Site Arrangements & Client Responsibilities :

  • Clients must ensure adequate facilities for the assigned photographer(s) and any accompanying staff, including seating, meals, and refreshments during long events.
    If the shoot is at a remote or outdoor location, the client must arrange for transport and accommodation for the photography team if required.
    Electricity and proper lighting arrangements must be provided at indoor venues to ensure optimal shooting conditions.

    • Photoshoot Timings & Extensions :

  • The photography team will adhere to the agreed time slots as per the package. If additional hours are required, extra charges will apply.
    Delays in the event schedule that extend beyond the agreed hours may lead to additional fees.

    • Image Delivery & Copyright Policy :

  • Edited photos and videos will be delivered within 2 to 6 weeks, depending on the package and workload.
    Clients will receive digital copies via a secure online gallery or USB drive, as agreed upon.
    Raw, unedited files will not be provided unless explicitly agreed upon in writing.
    Studio97 holds copyright ownership of all images taken and reserves the right to use them for promotional purposes (e.g., portfolio, website, social media).
    If a client wishes to keep their images private, a privacy clause must be agreed upon before the shoot.

    • Client Conduct & Cooperation :

  • Clients must cooperate with the photography team to ensure smooth execution of the shoot.
    If any member of the wedding party or guests behaves in a manner that threatens the safety of the photographers or their equipment, Studio97 reserves the right to terminate services immediately without a refund.

    • Weather & Force Majeure Policy :

  • In the case of unforeseen circumstances such as extreme weather, natural disasters, or other emergencies, Studio97 will offer rescheduling options based on availability.
    If rescheduling is not possible, a partial refund may be provided at the studio's discretion

    • Editing & Retouching :

  • Standard editing includes color correction, lighting adjustments, and minor retouching.
    Special editing requests (e.g., advanced retouching, background changes) will be subject to additional charges.

    • Equipment & Damage Liability :

  • Any intentional damage to Studio97’s equipment caused by the client or their guests will result in full compensation for repair or replacement.

    • Agreement & Acknowledgment :

  • Booking Studio97’s services signifies that the client has read, understood, and agreed to these terms and conditions.
